PatientTechnology

About:

This Podcast will give an overview of the biosimilars market. Industry experts will discuss regulatory controls, pricing pressure, the state of the healthcare industry, and other factors affecting the uptake of biosimilars in the market. Manufacturing challenges as well as current and future needs will also be discussed. Experts will also highlight technological innovations, such as digitalization technologies, and their role in facilitating analytical characterization and manufacturing controls for biosimilars.
